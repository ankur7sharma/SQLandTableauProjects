Sales are going down, so show following visualizations to see what's happening:
(i) Revenue breakdown by cities
(ii) Revenue breakdown by years and months
(iii) Top 5 customers by revenue
(iv) Top 5 products by revenue

Purpose of Project : To unlock sales insights that are not visible for sales team for decision support & automate them to reduce manual time sspent in data gathering.

End Result : An automated dashboard providing quick & latest sales insights in order to support data driven decision making.
